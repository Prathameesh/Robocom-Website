About Nothing:
Nothing Technology Limited (stylised as NOTHING) is a consumer technology company based in London, England.[1] It was founded by Carl Pei, the co-founder of OnePlus. Investors in the company include iPod inventor Tony Fadell, Twitch co-founder Kevin Lin, Reddit CEO Steve Huffman, and YouTuber Casey Neistat.[2] On 25 February 2021, the company announced Teenage Engineering as a founding partner, mainly responsible for the brand's design aesthetic and its products. Nothing's first product "ear (1)" was launched on 27 July 2021.
